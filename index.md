## Edge Types Detected

### 1. Liquidity Imbalance
Markets where order book depth shows significant asymmetry between YES/NO sides, creating arbitrage opportunities.

### 2. Volume Anomalies
Unusual trading activity that may indicate informed trading or market manipulation.

### 3. Price Inefficiencies
Probability estimates that diverge from fundamental analysis or external data sources.

## Methodology

Our edge detection system combines:

1. **Multi-Factor Scoring** - Weighted analysis of liquidity, volume, and price metrics
2. **Historical Backtesting** - Validation against past market outcomes
3. **Risk Assessment** - Position sizing recommendations based on edge confidence
4. **Real-Time Updates** - Hourly scans to catch emerging opportunities
